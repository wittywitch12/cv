Pizza

veg pizza

Ingredients

  • pizza base around 9 inch dia--1
  • pizza sauce--2 tbsp
  • cubbed onions,capsicum
    paneer
  • black olives
  • chilli flakes
  • mixed herbs
  • salt
  • mozzarella cheese
  • cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Spread pizza sauce generously and evenly over the pizza base.
  2. Arrange cubed vegetables,paneer,black olives over it.
  3. Top it with cheese blend evenly.
  4. sprinkle with chilli flakes,mixed herbes and salt if required and place it in wire rack
  5. preheat the oven to 180 degree celcius for 10 min.
  6. place the wire plate in oven and cook
  7. check for the doneness and remove it using tongs.
  8. serve it hot.
