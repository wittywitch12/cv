Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 1/4 cup maida
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2tsp baking soda
  • a pinch of salt
  • 1/4 cup walnuts
  • 3/4 cup choco chips

Instructions

  1. cream butter and sugar until soft and fluffy
  2. add vanilla essence,egg and whisk well.
  3. add all dry powders,walnutus,choco chips and
    combine well with a spatula.
  4. preheat the oven to 180 degree celcius for 10 min.
  5. divide the dough into equal portions.
  6. shape them into cookies and place on a greased baking tray and cook.
  7. check for doneness and remove the tray.
  8. let it cool for sometime.
